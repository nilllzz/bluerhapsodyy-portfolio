Commissioning Process

Contact me

Get in contact with me about your commission idea.

Use my contact methods:

  Discord DM   Bluesky DM   Instagram DM   Email

Reach agreement

We discuss the details and pricing of your commission and reach an agreement.

Before any work commences, you will sign my terms of service.

Deposit payment

If agreed upon, you will make a deposit (partial upfront payment) with the agreed payment method.

This payment is still refundable and commission details can still be changed.

Payment methods: PayPal, Zelle, Venmo, Cash App.

Sketch

I will show you a sketch of your commission.

If this sketch is to your liking, the previous deposit is no longer refundable, and any significant changes to the artwork after this point may incur additional costs.

Progress updates

I will deliver regular updates on your commission.

Feedback and small changes can be incorporated into the final artwork.

Artwork shared will be watermarked.

Final Artwork & Payment

I will show you the final watermarked artwork, and you will make the final payment.

Once I received the payment, I will send you the final, unwatermarked artwork.
